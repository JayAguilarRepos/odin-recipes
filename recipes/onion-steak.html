Onion Steak Recipe

Description

This is a delicions quick-to-make latin style dish that consists of thinly slinced eye round steak cooked with onions for a sweet and savory taste.

Ingreadients

  • 1 lbs of eye round steak
  • One spanish onion
  • Adobo Goya seasoning
  • Minced garlic
  • Olive oil
  • White vinager

Steps

  1. Place the thinly sliced eye round steak in a deep bowl.
  2. Add a tablespoon of Adobo Goya seasoning to the steak.
  3. Add a tablespoon of minced garlic to the steak.
  4. Add two tablespoons of vinegar to the steak.
  5. Mix the ingredients thoroughly with the steak.
  6. Slice the onion and then pour a teaspoon of salt on the slices.
  7. Heat a pan in medium heat for five minutes.
  8. Pour two table spoons of olive oil in the heated pan.
  9. Saute the onions for a minute or two.
  10. Cook the steak for three minutes on each side.
