Cuban Chicken Stew Recipe

Description

This is a delicions quick-to-make cuban style dish that consists of savory chicken drums sticks cooked with typical cuban cuiseine ingrediants.

Ingreadients

  • ~1.5 lbs of chicken drum sticks
  • One orange
  • One lime
  • One diced spanish onion
  • Half diced red pepper
  • Half diced green pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped potatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cooking wine
  • Olive oil
  • Olives
  • Bay leaves
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Place the chicken durm sticks in a deep bowl.
  2. Squeeze the lime and the orange on the chiken.
  3. add a table spoon of minced garlic.
  4. add Adobo Goya to the chicken.
  5. Preheat the pot and add olive oil to the pot.
  6. Brown the chicken (five minutes each side).
  7. Remove the chicken from the pot (do not taste as it is not cooked).
  8. Saute the diced onions, green peppers, and red peppers.
  9. Pour a cup of tomato sauce into the pot.
  10. Pour a cup of cooking wine into the pot.
  11. Add bayleaves and olives to the sauce.
  12. When the sauce boils, reintroduce the chicken and cook for 30 minutes
  13. Add the diced potatoe and cook for another 20 minutes
  14. Serve!
