Guava Pastry Recipe

Description

This is a delicions quick-to-make cuban pastry to go along any other meal as a first calss desert

Ingreadients

  • Guava paste
  • Puff pastry dough
  • cane sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Slice guava paste into cubes.
  3. Slice puff pastry dough into correspoding cubes.
  4. Stuff the puff pastry slices with guava paste.
  5. Bake the guava pastry for 25 minutes.
  6. Enjoy desert
